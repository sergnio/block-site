# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 10, 2026

Website Blocker ("the extension") does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

## What the extension stores

The extension stores only the list of website domains you choose to block. This list is saved using Chrome's built-in `storage.sync` API, which keeps the data:

- Locally in your browser, and
- Synced across your own Chrome installations via your Google account (if Chrome Sync is enabled).

This data never leaves Google's sync infrastructure and is never sent to the extension developer or any third party.

## What the extension does NOT do

- No analytics or telemetry.
- No tracking of browsing activity.
- No remote servers contacted by the extension.
- No ads.
- No accounts, subscriptions, or payments.

## Permissions

- `declarativeNetRequest` — used to block requests to the domains you specify.
- `storage` — used to save your blocked-domains list (see above).
